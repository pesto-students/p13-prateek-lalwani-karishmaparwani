Nanded, Maharashtra

Location

Nanded is located on the banks of river Godavari in west-central India.

About

Weather

Nanded has tropical wet and dry climate with dry conditions prevailing for most of the year. It receives about 163 mm of rainfall in June.

Population

Nanded population is estimated to be 4,672,196 in 2023.
